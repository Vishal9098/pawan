Our Step-by-Step House Cleaning Process

At Mad Dog Carpet and Tile Cleaning, we follow a detailed and systematic house cleaning process to ensure your home is spotless, fresh, and inviting. Here’s how we do it:

  1. Initial Consultation: We start by understanding your specific cleaning needs and preferences to create a customized plan.

  2. Decluttering: Our team tidies up your space, putting items in their proper places to make cleaning more efficient.

  3. Dusting and Wiping: We dust all surfaces, including shelves, furniture, and fixtures, and wipe down countertops, appliances, and windowsills.

  4. Vacuuming and Mopping: We thoroughly vacuum carpets, rugs, and upholstery, and mop hard floors to remove dirt and grime.

  5. Deep Cleaning: For kitchens and bathrooms, we scrub sinks, sanitize countertops, clean appliances, and disinfect high-touch areas.

  6. Final Touches: We add finishing touches like polishing mirrors, arranging furniture, and ensuring every corner of your home looks its best.

  7. Quality Check: Our team conducts a final inspection to ensure every area meets our high standards of cleanliness.

By following this step-by-step process, we guarantee a clean, fresh, and comfortable home for you and your family. Let us handle the hard work while you enjoy the results!
