Top Tips for Keeping Your Furniture Looking New

  • Vacuum Often: Weekly vacuum prevents dust buildup.
  • Blot Spills Fast: Dab immediately to avoid stains.
  • Flip Cushions: Rotate cushions to prevent uneven wear.
  • Use Fabric Protector: Helps repel stains.
  • Keep Out of Sunlight: Prevent fading.
  • Get It Professionally Cleaned: Deep clean occasionally.
  • Add Covers: Great for pets and kids.
